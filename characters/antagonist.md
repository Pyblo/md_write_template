# Antagonist Profile

## Name: Dr. Sarah Chen

## Basic Information

- Age: 35
- Occupation: Time Research Scientist
- Location: New York City

## Personality Traits

- **Positive**: Brilliant, determined, strategic
- **Negative**: Ruthless, manipulative, obsessed with her goals

## Background Story

Dr. Chen was once a colleague of John's at the university where they both worked on time theory research. She was considered brilliant but also controversial for her experiments.

## Character Arc

**Beginning**: Dr. Chen is researching time manipulation for scientific advancement.
**Middle**: She discovers that John has abilities and wants to study them.
**End**: She tries to control or exploit John's powers for her own purposes.

## Key Relationships

- **Rival**: John (has a complex relationship with his former colleague)
- **Ally**: Dr. Martinez (another researcher who supports her goals)

## Important Details

- Has a research lab in Manhattan
- Has been studying time manipulation for over 10 years
- Is willing to sacrifice ethics for scientific progress
