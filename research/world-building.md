# World Building Research

## Time Manipulation Rules

### Basic Principles

- Time can be slowed down or sped up
- The more complex the manipulation, the more energy required
- There are physical limitations to how much time can be changed

### Consequences

- Mental fatigue from constant use
- Memory gaps when time is manipulated
- Potential for creating paradoxes

## Setting Details

### New York City

- **Climate**: Temperate with distinct seasons
- **Culture**: Fast-paced, diverse, urban
- **Technology**: High-tech environment with traditional elements

### Character Environment

- John's apartment: Small but cozy
- Work environment: Modern office with open spaces
- Social spaces: Coffee shops and parks in Brooklyn

## Research Questions

1. How does time manipulation feel physically?
2. What are the social implications of having time powers?
3. How do different people react to time changes?
