# Scene 1: The Morning Routine

## Location

John's small apartment in Brooklyn

## Time

6:30 AM

## Characters Present

- John Smith

## Scene Summary

John wakes up and begins his morning routine. He makes coffee, checks his phone, and prepares for work.

## Detailed Description

John's alarm clock buzzes. He reaches over to turn off the alarm, stretching his arms. The familiar scent of coffee fills the air as he pours water into the machine. His phone shows a message from Sarah: "Morning! How are you doing?"

## Key Details

- Coffee maker is an old Keurig that John bought secondhand
- His apartment has a view of a brick wall
- The morning light filters through his curtains

## Dialogue

**John**: "Good morning, Whiskers." (to his cat)

## Emotional Beats

John feels content with his routine. He's comfortable in his small space, but wonders if there's more to life.
