# Scene 2: The Discovery

## Location

John's apartment, later the subway

## Time

7:45 AM

## Characters Present

- John Smith

## Scene Summary

John experiences his first time manipulation, accidentally changing the flow of time.

## Detailed Description

John is walking to work when he notices something odd. His phone shows a different time than his watch. He stops and looks around, confused. Suddenly, he realizes that everything has slowed down.

## Key Details

- The coffee shop counter seems frozen in time
- People are moving in slow motion
- John's heart pounds as he tries to understand what's happening

## Dialogue

**John**: "What is happening? Is this a dream?"

## Emotional Beats

John feels scared and confused. He realizes he has changed something fundamental about the world around him.

## Writing Notes

This scene should be mysterious and exciting, showing John's confusion while also establishing that his abilities are real.
